# Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

## Overview

PilotCurrency is designed to help aircrew track currency items, review planning tools, and access reference resources. This app does not sell your data, does not show advertising, and does not use third-party analytics or tracking SDKs.

## Information the App Stores

PilotCurrency stores currency-entry data locally on your device so your log entries remain available between app launches.

This may include:

- Logged currency events
- Event dates
- Selected crew position and entry preferences

At this time, that data is stored locally on-device using Apple platform storage and is not automatically synced to a remote PilotCurrency server.

## Information the App Does Not Collect

PilotCurrency does not intentionally collect or transmit personal information such as:

- Name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Precise location
- Contacts
- Photos
- Advertising identifiers

PilotCurrency also does not use in-app advertising, behavioral tracking, or third-party analytics.

## Network Requests

The app may make limited network requests when you use features that depend on external services.

Examples include:

- Aviation weather lookups used for planning features
- Opening external web content inside the app or in a web view

Those requests are made only to support the feature you selected. External websites and services operate under their own privacy practices and terms.

## External Websites and Services

Some app features open third-party or organization-managed websites, forms, or tools. When you use those features, your interaction with those services is governed by the privacy policy and terms of the external site, not this app alone.

Examples may include:

- ORM tools
- ASAP reporting
- Google Forms
- Other linked squadron or operational resources

## Data Sharing

PilotCurrency does not sell, rent, or trade your information.

The app does not share your locally stored currency data with the developer except where you intentionally interact with an external site or service from within the app.

## Data Retention

Locally stored app data remains on your device until:

- You delete entries
- You remove the app
- Your device or simulator data is reset

Because data is currently stored locally, deleting the app may delete your stored records.

## Security

PilotCurrency relies on the security protections provided by the Apple platform for locally stored data. No method of storage or transmission is guaranteed to be completely secure.

## Children’s Privacy

PilotCurrency is not directed to children under 13 and is intended for operational or professional use.

## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be reflected by revising the "Last updated" date above.

## Contact

If you want to provide feedback or request changes to this Privacy Policy, add the appropriate contact information for the app owner, maintainer, or organization before publishing.
